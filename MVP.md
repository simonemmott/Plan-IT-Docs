# Plan IT Minimum Viable Product
As a minimum Plan IT allows a user to define a project as a set of milestones and their relationships.
It presents these milestones visually with an estimated completion date of the project.
It assists project delivery by linking the objectives for a sprint to the milestones of a project and
tracks a sprints achievements against those objectives until the milestones are completed.
it presents a report of the sprint outputs for the projects stakeholders.

## Milestones
Milestones define the objectives of a project and when they are expected to be completed,
They have a planned duration and a planned completion date.
They may have a actual start date and an actual completion date.
They have a unique name within the project.
They have completion criteria which must be achieved to complete the milestone.
They may have a description.
Milestones may have a parent milestone.
A milestone may require other milestones to complete before it can start.
A milestone may require other milestones to start before it can start.
A milestone may require other milestones to complete before it can complete.
